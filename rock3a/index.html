More Interfaces for Your Diverse Application Needs

One M.2 E-Key connector

One M.2 E-Key with PCIe 2.0 interface, SDIO interface, UART interface, and USB interface, enabling high-speed WiFi/BT connectivity and support for other standard M.2 wireless modules.

One Gigabit Ethernet

One Gigabit Ethernet with Power over Ethernet (PoE) support, powered by PoE with an additional HAT required.

One M.2 M Key connector

One M.2 M‑Key Connect supporting up to 8TB M.2
NVMe SSD with 2‑lane PCIe 3.0 interface
enabling high speed storage access.
